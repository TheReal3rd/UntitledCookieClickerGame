# UntitledCookieClickerGame
![Image of the Administrations crest.](/Crest.gif)

3D Cookie Clicker game witha retro CRT theme within a 1984 like world.

You're a forced to preduce cookies for the party and must meet deadlines and demands and forced to take decisions that could lead further trouble later down the line.
