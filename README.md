## Lore:
The game is set in 2044 post war, a war that suffered great loss of life. As if no one ever learns there is another war raiging not for resource but for control.

1. Player - You're a apart of the work force. You where human. Now you work for the Administration.
2. Maan - "The Ministry of Agriculture and Nutrition" the parties body to control and maintain food and water rations.
3. Army - "The Department of War" The body that handles war and defence related tasks.
4. Energy - "The Ministry of Coal and Energy" Their primary source of power is coal but studies for nuclear have begun its like learning old tech again.
5. Information - "The Ministry of Information" The source of the news reels and papers. The only source for "Truthful" and "Unscensored" information.
6. Police - "The Ministry of Enforcement" The policing authority of the nation. Holding the highest form of power and control. Avoid them much as possible.
7. Medical - "The Ministry of Health" The medical bay of your district. Provides health updates of the population and army.

### Story:
The game is set in 2044 post war that started after revolution within 2029 in which a old world nation called "United Kingdom" collased after an attempted coup.
In which lead to a civil conflict which leaked to nearby nations from "United states" to the "CCCP" leading to a nuclear exchange obliterating large population density areas.
Deveopment in cybernetics lead to "humanities" survival leading to redevelopment technology. Ability to operate in contaminated districts.

The player will be operating within their working Facility called Unit 287 District 9 (U287D9) where they will manufacture cookies and meet deadlines.
You will work with a Unit 8897 known as to you as MAAN your manager and director.
